What is rate my professor?

I know you've seen all those people saying make sure you use rate my professor before picking classes in your upcoming semester,what exactly is rate my professor?

Rate my professor is a free website where students can go to and search for professors before adding them to their shopping cart. This is important becuase other than you and your strength, you need a teacher who is willing to support you throught the semester,you need to Find a teacher who wants to help you find a study schedule and a teacher who will meet with you during office hours,that's what rate my professor is really good for.

Not only can you view professors for classes that you want to take but you can also leave reviews after you have taken the professor and this webiste will show you how and why leaving a review is important for future students on making either the best or worst choice on their upcoming semester.

This website will show you how to use Ratemyprofessors.com,how to view the top professors, and finally how to leave your own reveiws so others can pick their future classes based off of how descriptive you were in your review.
